Sustainable Tourism

Eco-friendly practices to preserve Boisar's beaches, nature, and local culture

Responsible Waste

Keep beaches clean by avoiding plastic use and disposing waste properly in designated areas.

Protect Nature

Respect coastal ecosystems, avoid disturbing wildlife, and preserve greenery during visits.

Support Local
