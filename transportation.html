Transportation Guide

Explore convenient and affordable transportation options to reach and travel around Boisar. Multiple modes of transport available for every budget and preference.

Quick Travel Info

Boisar is well-connected via railway, road, and has good proximity to Mumbai's Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport. Plan your journey based on your starting point and preferred mode of travel.

Railway Transport

Boisar Railway Station

Western Railway Line - Main hub for Boisar
Direct trains from Mumbai Central (1.5-2 hours)
Local & Express trains available
Station amenities: Ticket counter, waiting area
Main Hub Most Connected Budget Friendly Daily Trains

Palghar Railway Station

Western Railway Line - District headquarters
Trains from Mumbai (1.5 hours), excellent frequency
Well-developed station infrastructure
Good boarding point for broader region access
District Hub Frequent Service Modern Amenities Reliable

Dahanu Road Railway Station

Western Railway Line - Gateway to Dahanu beaches
1 hour 45 minutes from Mumbai
Auto/taxi easily available for Dahanu Beach (10 km)
Less crowded, convenient for beach lovers
Beach Access 1 Hour 45 Min Scenic Route Quiet Station

Virar Railway Station

Northern gateway - Entry point to Boisar region
Closest to Mumbai (55 minutes)
Good transport links to Boisar, Palghar, and inland areas
Busiest station with most frequent services
Closest to Mumbai 55 Minutes Highest Frequency Busy Hub
